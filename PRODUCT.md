# HR-Agent

register: product

## Product Purpose
AI hiring copilot. Recruiter uploads a resume PDF, optionally a job posting URL or pasted JD text. A multi-agent pipeline (parser, GitHub scout, JD analyst, evaluator) returns an explainable score breakdown and a JD fit assessment.

## Users
Technical recruiters and hiring engineers screening candidate resumes. Desktop, office daylight, quick decisions between other tasks.

## Brand & Tone
Calm, precise, evidence-first. The interface should feel like a careful analyst's desk, not a flashy SaaS dashboard. Numbers earn trust through evidence text next to them.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS gradient dashboards
- Neon "AI" purple glows
- Hero-metric cards with giant numbers and no context

## Strategic Principles
- One screen, one flow: upload -> analyze -> read results.
- Every score shows its evidence.
- Waiting state must communicate which agent is working.
