# Do Apply

register: product

## Product Purpose
Do Apply (doapply.online) is a job-seeker's AI copilot for the whole hunt: analyze and score a resume, build a JD-tailored version, import a LinkedIn profile, search and match jobs with fast two-stage scoring, track companies, find recruiters (by natural-language query), and draft truthful outreach. A multi-agent backend does the work; the frontend keeps the loop tight: find, tailor, reach out.

## Users
Job seekers, mostly engineers and technical people, running their search on a laptop in focused sessions. They want signal fast: which jobs fit, what to fix, who to contact. Secondary: the same person casually checking new matches between other tasks.

## Brand & Tone
Decisive and clean, with momentum. Do Apply is about taking action, not admiring dashboards. Confident but never loud. Numbers and matches always carry their reasoning. The landing page should feel like a sharp modern product (Linear/Vercel calibre of craft), not a template.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS gradient-mesh hero with three identical feature cards
- Neon "AI" purple glow
- Navy-and-gold fintech cliche
- Centered icon-title-subtitle card grids

## Strategic Principles
- Tight loop: search -> tailor -> reach out, few clicks between.
- Every score and match shows its reasoning.
- Latency is a feature: instant results first, LLM refinement second.
- Truthful by construction: outreach and tailoring never fabricate.
